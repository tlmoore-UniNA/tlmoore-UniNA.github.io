nanoHTFS

Nanomedicine High Throughput Formulation Screening

We are researchers in the Department of Pharmacy at the University of Naples Federico II, and part of the Delivery Technology (DeliTech) research unit. Our goal is to develop nanomedicines for the delivery of active therapeutic agents, such as small molecules or nucleic acids, to treat various pathologies.

To do so, we implement automation for high-throughput nanoparticle development, as well as in vitro screening. We further utilize advanced computational methods to build machine learning models that can facilitate and optimize our development process.
